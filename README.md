# TravanaAI 🔮 - Premium Travel Companion & AI Planner

TravanaAI is a premium, state-of-the-art travel planning prototype that helps you craft custom adventures. It automatically designs detailed itineraries, estimates hotel rates, locates local cuisines, curates shopping guidelines, and outlines visual map routes based on your preferences.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **🔮 Conversational Smart Wizard**: Answer a quick 8-step questionnaire detailing your destination, starting point, companions, age group, style, budget, and trip duration to get a custom itinerary.
*   **🗺️ Interactive Split-Pane Itinerary**: View a dynamic visual route map alongside scrollable timeline events, hotel tier pricing (Budget, Best Value, Premium), and local culinary highlights.
*   **🔥 Swipeable Vibe Matcher**: Browse matching getaways in a Tinder-style swipe deck. Swipe right to save a place to your wishlist, or left to skip.
*   **📍 Local Getaways slider**: Instantly view and plan weekend trips close to home (including Rishikesh, Shimla, Kasol, Manali, Jaipur, and more).
*   **👑 Personalized Recommendation Cycle**: Get cycling suggestions based on your previous activity that change on every visit to keep your homepage fresh.
*   **📋 Travel Companion Profile**: Keep track of your travel levels and edit your personal details (Full Name, Phone, Email) with a glassmorphic form that syncs dynamically.
*   **🦊 Cute Mascot Graphics**: Interact with Pip, your travel mascot fox, who validates your inputs in real-time and animates dynamically during packing loader sequences.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **HTML5 & CSS3 Variables**: Core structure and styling tokens (supporting Cyber Neon, Sakura Cream, and Sunset Gold vibes).
*   **Vanilla ES6 JavaScript**: Interactive router, gesture handlers, swiping mechanisms, and itinerary generation logic.
*   **FontAwesome Icons**: High-fidelity modern iconography.
*   **Glassmorphism styling**: Premium translucent frosted-glass containers, glow boundaries, and modern animations.
